# Autonomous Nexus: Comprehensive User Guide

Welcome to the **Autonomous Nexus Spatial Operating System**. This guide covers the operation of the platform across Desktop (Windows/Linux) and Mobile (iOS/Android).

---

## 1. Platform Navigation (The Spatial OS)

The traditional web dashboard has been replaced by the **Infinite Canvas**. 

### Desktop (Windows/Linux)
*   **The Omnibar**: Press `Cmd + K` (Mac) or `Ctrl + K` (Windows/Linux) from anywhere to open the Global Command Palette. Type commands like "Recon", "Quantum", or "Billing".
*   **Zenith HUD**: Type "Zenith" into the Omnibar to strip away all borders and enter a distraction-free, full-screen mode. Press `ESC` to exit.
*   **Navigation**: Click and drag to pan across 3D maps. Scroll wheel to zoom.

### Mobile (iOS/Android)
*   **The Omnibar**: Tap the glowing cyan search icon floating at the top of your screen to open the Omnibar.
*   **Gestures**: 
    *   *Pinch-to-zoom*: Zoom in and out of the 3D Holographic War Room and Target Portals.
    *   *Two-finger swipe*: Rotate 3D models (like the Quantum Qubit Matrix).
    *   *Bottom Nav*: Use the persistent bottom navigation bar to switch between Arsenal, Portals, and Quantum modules.

## 2. Core Modules

### A. Adaptive Ops (Generative UI)
The UI rebuilds itself based on the operation:
*   **Web Audit**: Displays an HTTP waterfall. Look for red nodes in the DOM tree indicating SQL injections.
*   **WiFi Hack**: Displays an RF Spectrum Analyzer. Watch for yellow spikes indicating a captured EAPOL handshake.
*   **OSINT**: Displays an interactive Identity Graph. Tap a node to expand intelligence on that specific target.

### B. Target Portals (Non-Euclidean Topology)
*   **Overview**: Represents compromised targets as glowing spheres.
*   **Action**: Double-click (Desktop) or Double-tap (Mobile) a portal sphere to fly the camera *inside* the target's filesystem. Click "Close Portal & Extract" at the bottom to exit.

### C. Quantum Cyber Warfare (Tier 5 Only)
Available exclusively to **Nexus Prime** subscribers.
*   **Shor's Decryption Engine**: Tap "Initialize Simulation". Watch the 3D Qubit Matrix align to instantly shatter RSA-4096 keys. The decrypted payload will appear in the bottom-left terminal.
*   **HNDL Cryo-Vault**: Stores unbreakable TLS 1.3 traffic in digital ice. Run the decryption module to shatter the ice and extract historical packet captures.

## 3. Subscriptions & Billing

Access to advanced modules requires security clearance upgrades.

1.  Navigate to the **Billing & Upgrade** tab via the sidebar (Desktop) or "More" menu (Mobile).
2.  Review the tiers:
    *   **Free Trial**: Basic recon and 1 Agent.
    *   **Operative**: Node playbooks and full arsenal.
    *   **Commander**: Generative UI and 3D Portals.
    *   **Nexus Prime**: Quantum Warfare suites.
3.  Select Monthly or Annual (saves 16%).
4.  Click **Select [Tier]** to be redirected to the secure Stripe checkout.
5.  Upon successful payment, your HUD will automatically update your clearance level in the status bar.
